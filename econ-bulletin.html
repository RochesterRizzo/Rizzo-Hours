Read this first

This page collects opportunities that may be useful to economics students. Some are formal jobs or internships; others are summer programs, research positions, fellowships, competitions, public-policy opportunities, or things that simply seem worth your attention.

I do not screen every opportunity in detail. Check eligibility, deadlines, location, compensation, application requirements, and whether it actually fits your goals.

Start early

Do not wait until senior year. Browse early, notice patterns, and start building the skills and evidence these opportunities ask for.

Verify details

Check dates carefully. If a deadline is near, go directly to the organization’s site and verify current application instructions.

Use the flyers

Many postings include flyer thumbnails. Click the flyer or the button to view or download the full file.

Research / Fellowships

Use this section for research assistant roles, department research opportunities, think-tank fellowships, data projects, grant-funded work, and academic-year opportunities.
